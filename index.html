About Us

"No child needs to stay in darkness."

💜 Why We Exist

Healing A Purple Heart Foundation exists to support children and young people through emotional healing, self-discovery, and purpose development. We believe no young person should have to grow through silence, confusion, or emotional pain without support.

📖 The Heart Behind the Foundation

Anchored in 2 Corinthians 1:3–4: "God is the Father of mercies and the God of all comfort — who comforts us in all affliction, so that we may also comfort others."

🎯 Our Mission

To provide mental health education, emotional support, talent discovery, skill development, and global opportunity access for children and adolescents.

💜 The Meaning of "Purple Heart"

Purple represents identity, uniqueness, and purpose. Heart represents emotion, humanity, and lived experience. Together: a healed, sensitive, and purpose-filled identity.

⭐ Core Values

Emotional safety Compassion & empathy Identity discovery Creative healing Purpose-driven growth

Meet Our Founder

Anita Nwokoji

Spoken word artist · Youth advocate · Mental health voice
Miss Gospel Africa 2025 · Founder, Healing A Purple Heart Foundation

This foundation was born from a deeply personal journey through trauma and emotional darkness. Writing and poetry became her way out. What started as survival became transformation. She believes no child should suffer in silence.

"No child needs to stay in darkness. Every child deserves to be seen, heard, and understood."
